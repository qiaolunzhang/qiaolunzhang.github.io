About me:

I received a double M.S. degree from Politecnico di Milano and Shanghai Jiao Tong University. I’m currently pursuing the Ph.D. degree in Politecnico di Milano under supervision of Prof. Massimo Tornatore. I also worked with Dr. Patricia Layec in Nokia Bell Labs France as a research intern.

My research interests mainly focus on resource allocation for optical networks using operations research.
